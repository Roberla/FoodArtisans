About Zara Photography

Who is Zara?

Specializing in food and beverage photography, I have been working headlining advertising and lifestyle shoots for eight years. I am based in Cable City, but frequently travel to locations all over the world. While I generally work alone with the styling and creative teams, I do have a small, hand-picked team of second-shooters and lighting specialists that I work with for bigger projects.

Why Zara?

  • Artisan approach
  • Mostly natural light
  • Earthy tones and textures
  • Minimal post-production for a more authentic product
